Welcome! This is LiKo, Linear Independence Korean learning tool.
How to learn Korean conversation? It's simple! Just follow these steps!
  1. Click one of the profiles on the left.
  2. Click the situation you want to study.
  3. While studying, click the bold words to see their meanings.
    Click Add to vocab to add them to vocabulary list.
  4. To study the words you added, click Vocab.
  5. After studying, click Test to review your learning.
Have a nice time with LiKo! !
